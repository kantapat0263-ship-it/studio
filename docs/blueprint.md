# **App Name**: Lotus Group Engineering

## Core Features:

- Header & Navigation: Prominently display the Lotus Group logo, primary slogan ('ENGINEERING EXCELLENCE FOR SOCIETY AND ENVIRONMENT OF THE WORLD'), contact information (address, phone numbers) from the provided image, and a single-page navigation.
- Company Profile: An 'About Us' section detailing the company's vision, mission, and general expertise in electrical, plumbing, air conditioning, and structural work.
- Services Overview: Dedicated sections outlining each core service area (electrical, plumbing, air conditioning, structural work) with concise descriptions of capabilities.
- Project Showcase: A curated gallery or list featuring a minimum set of key projects, with images and brief titles, to demonstrate company experience and quality of work.
- Affiliated Companies: Display the logos and names of affiliated companies as shown in the original header image, reinforcing the group's network.
- Contact Information & Form: Clearly present physical address and phone numbers for direct communication, along with a simple contact form for inquiries.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A dark olive green (#545C3D) to reflect the 'military green' brand identity, suitable for key elements and strong text.
- Background Color: A very dark grey (#1B1D16) with a subtle green tint, providing a sophisticated, technical, and professional backdrop for the content.
- Accent Color: A subtle gold-yellow (#D9C68C) for highlighting interactive elements, icons, and providing a touch of elegance, inspired by elements in the provided header image.
- Headlines and body text: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, techy, and precise feel that aligns with an engineering company's professionalism.
- Utilize clean, geometric line-art icons that match the Lotus Group logo's style and represent engineering, construction, and contact actions.
- A clean, single-page layout with clearly defined, scrolling sections. The design should emphasize professionalism and clear information hierarchy.
- Implement subtle, smooth transition animations for scrolling between sections and understated hover effects for interactive components, maintaining a professional demeanor.
